About Me

Hey this is me, I am a very mercurial guy, and I can stay in the same thing for too long, I tend to get bored easily with simple stuff, that's probably why I've always liked challenging things, like puzzles, logic and strategy games, I am an amateur chess player, my older brother taught me to play when I was 5 years old, and since that I have played some tournament ocassionally. I am also a Dota 2 player, I think Dota 2 is one of the most exciting games out there. Finally one of my other passions is science, specially math connected problems, right now I am pursuing a PhD at Purdue University under the supervision of Tamal Dey. Prior to coming here I worked at a startup, Amphora Health, for almost 3 years, I was involved in population genomics projects, as well data-driven healthcare insights, it was a wonderful place to work, where I met extraordinary people. I would like to apply my learnings discovering data-driven healthcare insights onwards.
